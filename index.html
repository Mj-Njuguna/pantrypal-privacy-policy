Privacy Policy

Pantry Pal - Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Commitment: Pantry Pal is built on a "Privacy First" architecture. We prioritize local storage to ensure your data remains under your control at all times.

1. Information We Collect

Pantry Pal collects the following data to provide its core services:

  • Device Identifier: A non-persistent unique ID used solely for app integrity and local data separation.
  • Inventory Content: Product names, categories, and expiry dates provided by the user.
  • Camera Data: Used in real-time for barcode scanning. No images are saved or transmitted.
  • App Performance Data: Anonymized usage statistics to diagnose crashes and improve functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Maintain your personal food inventory locally.
  • Generate local notifications for expiring items.
  • Match barcodes with product names via third-party APIs.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption in Transit: Any data sent externally (such as barcode lookups) is encrypted using modern cryptographic protocols (HTTPS).
  • Local Storage: All inventory data is stored in a secure local database on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not automatically sync your data to any cloud servers.
  • No Account Required: We do not require usernames, passwords, or email registration.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of services to enhance the app:

  • OpenFoodFacts API: Used to identify products. No personal data is shared with this service.
  • Supabase (Client-Side): Used as a local data management library. Data remains on your device.

5. Data Deletion & Rights

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, you have full control over your data:

  • Immediate Deletion: You can delete any item or the entire database within the app settings.
  • Automatic Deletion: Uninstalling the application will permanently remove all locally stored pantry data from your device.

6. Permissions

  • Camera: Required for barcode scanning functionality.
  • Notifications: Required to alert you about expiring food items.

7. Children's Privacy

Pantry Pal does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us for immediate removal.

8. International Data Transfers

As data is stored locally on your device, no international transfers of your personal information occur.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below.
