Privacy Policy

Pantry Pal - Your Privacy Matters

Privacy First: Pantry Pal is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never share your information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Pantry Pal collects and processes the following information:

  • Device Identifier: A unique identifier generated for your device to keep your pantry data separate and secure
  • Pantry Items: Food items you add, including names, categories, and expiry dates
  • Camera Access: Used exclusively for barcode scanning to identify products
  • App Usage Data: Basic functionality usage to improve the app experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Pantry Pal service:

  • Store and manage your pantry inventory locally on your device
  • Send you notifications about expiring items
  • Scan barcodes to automatically identify products
  • Provide personalized app functionality

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • Local Storage: All your pantry data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up
  • Secure Database: Data is stored in a secure local database with device-level encryption
  • No Account Required: No personal accounts, passwords, or login credentials needed

Permissions We Request

Pantry Pal requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan product barcodes for easy item entry
  • Notifications: To alert you about items nearing expiry
  • Storage: To save your pantry data locally on your device

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • OpenFoodFacts API: To retrieve product information from barcodes (no personal data shared)
  • Supabase: For local database functionality (data remains on your device)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data may only be disclosed if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety
  • You explicitly consent to sharing

Children's Privacy

Pantry Pal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Remove individual items or clear all data
  • Control: Disable notifications or camera access at any time
  • Uninstall: Completely remove the app and all associated data

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Delete individual items or clear all data
  • Uninstall the application
  • Reset your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains in your physical possession at all times.
